Sisi G Recipe

Description

A dish so good and rich that it induces guilt when consumed.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pig Head
  • Calamansi
  • Chopped Onions
  • Chilis
  • Margarine
  • Egg
  • Chicken Liver
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Grill the chopped pig's face.
  2. Warm up the sizzling pan and apply margarine.
  3. Place the grilled pork onto the sizzling pan then add the remaining ingredients.
  4. Add the egg on top, mix, then enjoy!
