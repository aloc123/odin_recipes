Shawarma Recipe

Description

An all-around no brainer meal that is saucy, meaty, crunchy, and fresh all in a bowl!

Ingredients

  • 900g Chicken Thighs
  • 1/4 cup Olive Oil
  • 2 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 3 teaspoon Taco Seasoning

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients to marinate the chicken.
  2. After 30 minutes, grill the chicken.
  3. Place it on top of white or java rice with lettuce, cucumber, tomato, onion, and the white sauce.
  4. Mix and enjoy!
